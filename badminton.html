Rules & Regulations

1. Scoring

  • A match consists of the best of 3 games to 21 points.
  • A point is scored on every rally (rally scoring).
  • At 20-20, the side that gains a 2-point lead first wins the game.
  • At 29-29, the side scoring the 30th point wins the game.

2. Serving

  • The serve must be hit underhand and below the server's waist.
  • The server and receiver must stand in diagonally opposite service courts.
  • The shuttlecock must land in the receiver's service court to be a valid serve.

3. Faults

  • If the shuttlecock lands outside the boundaries of the court.
  • If a player touches the net with their racket or body during play.
  • If the shuttlecock is hit twice in succession by the same player.
