Rules & Regulations

1. General Gameplay & Teams

  • Objective: The goal is to score points by sending the ball over the net so it lands within the opponent's court.
  • Teams: Games are played between two teams. The number of players on the court will be decided per match.
  • Scoring: We will use rally scoring. A point is awarded on every rally, regardless of which team served. Sets are typically played to 25 points, and a team must win by at least a two-point margin.

2. Hitting & Attacks (Custom Rules)

  • Consecutive Hits: A single player is allowed to hit the ball twice in a row.
